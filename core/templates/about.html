About Srihari Developers

Building trust, creating communities, and delivering excellence for over 15 years

Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform the landscape of Tirupati, Srihari Developers began its journey in 2009 with a commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. What started as a small construction company has grown into one of the most trusted names in real estate development.

Our founder, Mr. P Srihari, envisioned creating not just buildings, but communities where families could thrive and grow. This philosophy continues to guide every project we undertake, ensuring that each development adds value to both residents and the surrounding community.

Today, we are proud to have delivered over 10+ successful projects, providing homes to more than 250 families while maintaining our commitment to quality craftsmanship and innovative design.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To create exceptional living and working spaces that enhance the quality of life for our customers while contributing to the sustainable development of our communities. We are committed to delivering projects that exceed expectations through innovation, quality craftsmanship, and unparalleled customer service.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred real estate developer in Andhra Pradesh, known for our integrity, innovation, and commitment to excellence. We envision a future where every family finds their perfect home through Srihari Developers, and every community is enriched by our developments.
